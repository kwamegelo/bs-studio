What Are Hybrid Lash Extensions?

Hybrid lash extensions combine both classic (1:1) and volume (2-5 fan) techniques in one application. This creates a textured, dimensional look that's fuller than classic but more natural than full volume, offering the perfect middle ground.

Care Instructions

Tips for Best Results

Pre-Appointment

  • Avoid oil-based products 24 hours before
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free lashes
  • Remove contact lenses if applicable

Aftercare

  • Avoid water, steam, and sweat for 24-48 hours
  • Use oil-free makeup remover only
  • Brush lashes daily with provided spoolie
  • Sleep on your back to avoid lash damage

Maintenance

  • Schedule touch-ups every 2-3 weeks
  • Avoid rubbing or pulling lashes
  • Use recommended lash cleanser
  • Avoid mascara when possible
Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Hybrid lashes combine both classic (1:1) and volume (fan) techniques in one set. You get single extensions on some lashes and 2-3 lash fans on others, creating a textured look that's fuller than classic but more natural than full volume.

Hybrid lashes are perfect for clients who want more fullness than classic extensions but find volume too dramatic. They're ideal for those with sparse lashes or anyone wanting a naturally enhanced everyday look with subtle glamour.

When applied correctly by a trained professional, hybrid lashes are safe for your natural lashes. We use proper isolation techniques and high-quality adhesives to ensure the health and integrity of your natural lashes.

Hybrid lashes typically last 3-4 weeks with proper care. We recommend touch-up appointments every 2-3 weeks to maintain fullness as your natural lashes naturally shed and new ones grow in.
