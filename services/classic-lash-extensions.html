What Are Classic Lash Extensions?

Classic lash extensions involve attaching a single synthetic lash to each of your natural lashes. This creates a natural, lengthened look that enhances your eyes without looking overly dramatic.

Care Instructions

Tips for Best Results

Pre-Appointment

  • Avoid oil-based products 24 hours before
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free lashes
  • Remove contact lenses if applicable

Aftercare

  • Avoid water, steam, and sweat for 24-48 hours
  • Use oil-free makeup remover only
  • Brush lashes daily with provided spoolie
  • Sleep on your back to avoid lash damage

Maintenance

  • Schedule touch-ups every 2-3 weeks
  • Avoid rubbing or pulling lashes
  • Use recommended lash cleanser
  • Avoid mascara when possible

Frequently Asked Questions

With proper care, classic lash extensions can last 3-4 weeks. Touch-ups are recommended every 2-3 weeks to maintain the full, beautiful look as your natural lashes naturally shed.

It's not necessary, but if you do choose to wear mascara, use only water-based formulas. Avoid oil-based or waterproof mascaras as they can break down the adhesive and reduce the lifespan of your extensions.

When applied correctly by a trained professional, lash extensions should not damage your natural lashes. We use proper techniques and high-quality adhesives to ensure the health of your natural lashes.

Come with clean lashes free of makeup, mascara, and oils. Avoid caffeine before your appointment as it can cause eye twitching. Remove contact lenses and plan for 90-120 minutes of relaxation time.
